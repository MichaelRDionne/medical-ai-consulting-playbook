# Recruiter Quick Scan

## What This Demonstrates

- Clinical judgment applied to AI product evaluation.
- Awareness of PHI, workflow boundaries, escalation, and human accountability.
- Ability to create reusable consulting artifacts, not just one-off demos.
- Communication style suitable for technical and non-technical stakeholders.

## What To Ask Me About

- How I decide whether a workflow should use AI at all.
- How I would evaluate a healthcare AI vendor.
- How I design human review gates.
- What clinical background adds to AI product review.
