# Human Review Plan

## Review Gate

Every AI-generated output must have a defined human owner before it can affect a patient, client, or operational record.

## Reviewer Checklist

- Confirm identity and context in the source system.
- Check whether the AI output omitted important information.
- Check whether the output overstates certainty.
- Confirm escalation criteria.
- Approve, edit, or reject the output.

## Audit Trail

- Store source reference.
- Store reviewer decision.
- Store final action.
- Store reason for override when applicable.
